What We Do

Our Services

From single-family homes to large commercial projects — delivered with the same precision and care every time.

01
Exterior Stucco

Full exterior applications for homes and commercial buildings. Three-coat systems, one-coat systems, and synthetic stucco (EIFS) installed to South Florida code standards.

02
Interior Plastering

Expert interior plaster finishes from smooth Level 5 to decorative Venetian. We match existing textures seamlessly and create new custom finishes to your exact specification.

03
Stucco Repair

Crack repair, patch work, and full section replacement. We identify underlying moisture issues and fix the root cause before recoating — no shortcuts, no recurring problems.

04
Custom Textures

From Spanish lace to Santa Barbara smooth, Mediterranean bands to modern sand finish. Tommy specialises in complex custom textures most contractors won't attempt.

05
New Construction

Preferred stucco sub for general contractors across Miami-Dade. We work on tight schedules, coordinate with other trades, and never hold up a project.

06
Commercial Projects

Office buildings, retail spaces, HOA common areas, and multi-family complexes. Large-scale commercial stucco with the precision and reliability commercial clients demand.
